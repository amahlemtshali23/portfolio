Pool House

YEAR: 2023

TYPE: residential


The brief of this project was to design a pool house for a client on the south side of the pool. They are tight on a budget. Therefore the structure may not exceed 36m². The pool house should include two change rooms, a covered outdoor bar area & built-in braai. One of the client's children has grown up and is attending university. They would like to extend the pool house to include an upstairs apartment. The pool house should be left unchanged and provide an outside staircase up to the first-floor apartment. The apartment must include a seating area, kitchenette, sleeping and studying area and a bathroom.
